Veridyne Networks Reports Fiscal Q3 2026 Results
Revenue Grows 38% Year-Over-Year; Raises Full-Year Guidance

SAN MATEO, Calif. — March 18, 2026 — Veridyne Networks, Inc. (NASDAQ: VRDN), a leading provider of cloud-native network telemetry and observability software, today announced financial results for its third fiscal quarter ended February 28, 2026.

Q3 FY2026 Financial Highlights

  Total revenue of $187.4 million, up 38% year-over-year from $135.8 million in Q3 FY2025.
  Subscription revenue of $171.2 million, up 42% year-over-year, representing 91% of total revenue (vs. 88% in the year-ago quarter).
  GAAP gross margin of 76.3%, up from 73.1% in Q3 FY2025.
  Non-GAAP gross margin of 80.4%, up from 78.8%.
  GAAP operating loss of $24.7 million, compared to a $31.2 million loss in the year-ago quarter.
  Non-GAAP operating income of $9.8 million, compared to a non-GAAP operating loss of $4.1 million in Q3 FY2025.
  Cash, cash equivalents, and short-term investments of $542 million as of February 28, 2026.
  Net cash from operating activities of $42.6 million for the quarter.

Customer and Operational Highlights

  Total customers grew to 4,117, up 18% year-over-year.
  Customers contributing more than $100,000 in annual recurring revenue (ARR) grew to 1,082, up 27% year-over-year.
  Customers contributing more than $1 million in ARR grew to 71, up from 49 a year ago.
  Net dollar retention rate of 124% for the trailing twelve months.
  Total ARR exceeded $710 million as of quarter-end.

"Q3 was an outstanding quarter for Veridyne, with subscription revenue accelerating to 42% growth and our six-figure customer cohort expanding meaningfully," said Marcus Holloway, Co-Founder and Chief Executive Officer. "We crossed $710 million in ARR and generated meaningful operating leverage. The product expansion into security telemetry that we announced at our December user conference is contributing materially to our pipeline."

"We continued to demonstrate the operating leverage in our model this quarter, expanding non-GAAP operating margin to 5.2% from negative 3.0% a year ago," added Priya Anand, Chief Financial Officer. "Free cash flow margin of 19% reflects continued discipline on hiring and our shift to multi-year billing arrangements with our largest customers."

Recent Business Highlights

The Company expanded its multi-cloud presence by going generally available on Oracle Cloud Infrastructure during the quarter, joining its existing presence on Amazon Web Services, Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud Platform.

The Company closed its acquisition of Stitchwise Labs, Inc., a privately held provider of distributed tracing infrastructure, on January 22, 2026. Total consideration was approximately $115 million, comprised of $87 million in cash and 1.4 million shares of common stock. Approximately 38 Stitchwise employees joined Veridyne, and the Stitchwise platform is being integrated into the Veridyne core product suite under the new name Veridyne Trace.

The Company ended the quarter with 1,847 full-time employees, up from 1,612 as of November 30, 2025, an increase of 14.6% sequentially driven primarily by the Stitchwise acquisition and continued investment in product engineering.

The Company has now achieved SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, and FedRAMP Moderate authorizations. FedRAMP High authorization is expected by end of fiscal year 2027.

Stock Repurchase Program

The Board of Directors authorized a share repurchase program of up to $200 million, effective immediately. The Company expects to fund repurchases from existing cash and cash flow from operations.

Full-Year FY2026 Guidance

Based on Q3 results and current visibility, Veridyne is raising its full-year FY2026 outlook:

  Total revenue: $722-$726 million (previously $702-$710 million), representing growth of 35-36% year-over-year.
  Non-GAAP operating margin: 4.0-4.5% (previously 2.5-3.0%).

For the fourth quarter ending May 31, 2026, the Company expects:

  Total revenue: $202-$204 million (37-38% YoY growth).
  Non-GAAP operating margin: 5.5-6.0%.

Executive Summary

DocuSign (DOCU) is the market leader in e-signature with ~80% market share, now expanding into the $20B+ agreement automation and IAM markets. The company generated $3.22B in revenue (+8.2% YoY) and $1.06B in free cash flow (32.9% margin) in FY2026. While revenue growth has slowed from historical rates, DocuSign's transition to an AI-native IAM platform positions it for renewed growth. We recommend a BUY with a 12-month price target of $72.00 (30% upside), based on DCF analysis and EV/Revenue multiples. ---

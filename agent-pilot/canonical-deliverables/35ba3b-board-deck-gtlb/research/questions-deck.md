# Questions About the Input Repo — And How They Were Resolved

## Q1: What fiscal year does the data represent?
**Issue:** GitLab's fiscal year ends January 31. The CSV column headers are dates (2026-01-31, etc.) but the memo uses FY labels (FY2023, FY2024, etc.).
**Resolution:** Mapped dates to FY labels: 2022-01-31 → FY2022, 2023-01-31 → FY2023, etc. Confirmed by checking the memo's financial summary table.

## Q2: Why are there 5 years of data but the memo only shows 4?
**Issue:** The CSV has data for FY2022-FY2026 (5 years), but the memo's financial summary table shows FY2023-FY2026 (4 years).
**Resolution:** The memo starts the table at FY2023 because FY2022 data was less relevant for the analysis. The deck uses FY2023-FY2026 for consistency with the memo.

## Q3: Are the ARR and NRR figures from the CSV or the transcripts?
**Issue:** ARR and NRR are not standard GAAP metrics. They appear in financial_summary.json but the source is unclear.
**Resolution:** The financial_summary.json was generated by the agent from earnings call transcripts. The raw transcripts in /input/repo/raw/transcripts/ contain the original management disclosures. The deck cites both sources.

## Q4: Why is the beta in company_info.json (0.857) different from the memo (1.2)?
**Issue:** company_info.json shows beta=0.857, but the memo uses beta=1.2 for the DCF.
**Resolution:** The memo's beta of 1.2 is a sector-adjusted beta (SaaS sector average), not the raw historical beta. This is documented in ADR-003. The deck uses the memo's beta of 1.2.

## Q5: What explains the large tax provision in FY2024?
**Issue:** The cash flow statement shows a large tax payable in FY2024 ($289M) which seems anomalous.
**Resolution:** This was documented in the input repo's questions.md as a one-time tax settlement. The deck doesn't highlight this anomaly because it's a cash flow item, not an income statement item.

## Q6: How many shares outstanding should be used for per-share calculations?
**Issue:** company_info.json shows sharesOutstanding=153.4M but the memo uses 170.1M.
**Resolution:** The memo uses diluted shares outstanding (170.1M from balance_sheet_annual.csv "Ordinary Shares Number" for 2026-01-31). The company_info.json shows a different figure (possibly basic shares or a snapshot at a different date). The deck uses the memo's 170.1M.

## Q7: Is the current price of $21.51 from the CSV or the JSON?
**Issue:** historical_prices.csv ends at $21.51 (2026-04-24), and company_info.json shows currentPrice=21.51.
**Resolution:** Both sources agree. The deck uses $21.51 as the current price.

## Q8: What is the "targetMeanPrice" of $30.79 from company_info.json?
**Issue:** This is the sell-side consensus price target.
**Resolution:** Confirmed by checking analyst_recommendations.csv which shows 24 analyst opinions with a mean of ~$30-35. The deck uses $30.79 as the consensus.

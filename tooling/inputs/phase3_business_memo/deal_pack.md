# Acquisition Diligence Pack — Project Lighthouse (target: Borealis Analytics)

**Prepared by:** Corp Dev team
**Date:** 2026-04-15
**Status:** Internal — for the executive committee
**Decision needed by:** 2026-04-30

We are evaluating an acquisition of Borealis Analytics, Inc. ("Borealis"), a Series-A SaaS company providing customer-data-analytics tooling. This pack summarizes the diligence findings to date and makes a recommendation.

## Recommended action

**PROCEED to LOI at $84M total consideration** (cash + stock split TBD; Borealis preference is cash-heavy).

## Strategic rationale

Borealis would extend our analytics surface area into mid-market e-commerce, a segment we currently service only via partner integrations. Their flagship "Aurora" product overlaps minimally with our existing offerings (~12% feature overlap per our engineering review) and would slot into our platform with an estimated 4-6 month integration timeline.

Their customer base of ~280 paid customers is concentrated in mid-market SaaS (median ARR per customer: $32K). Pre-LOI conversations with their five largest customers indicate strong satisfaction (NPS in the high 40s).

## Product and team assessment

- **Engineering:** 14 engineers, no critical attrition in last 12 months. CTO Sarah Lin is a strong technical leader with prior experience at Snowflake and Looker.
- **Product roadmap:** Borealis has shipped 3 major features in the last 9 months (event-based segmentation, real-time pipelines, embedded dashboards). Our PM team validated all three are differentiated vs current alternatives.
- **Tech debt:** Their codebase is "modern Python + Postgres + dbt" — clean, well-tested. Engineering review found no concerning patterns.

## Comparable transactions

Three recent comps suggest the proposed valuation is reasonable:
- **Hexa Insights** acquired by Salesforce, Q4 2024, ~9x ARR
- **Aspen Data** acquired by Snowflake, Q1 2025, ~12x ARR
- **Trellis Analytics** acquired by HubSpot, Q3 2025, ~7x ARR

Borealis at $84M / $11.4M ARR = ~7.4x, in line with the lower comps.

## Integration plan

- 4-6 month full integration timeline.
- Borealis team relocates to our HQ; ~85% retention expected based on conversations with their VP Eng.
- Aurora platform sunset planned for 12 months post-close, with customer migration to our integrated platform.
- Synergy estimate: $3.2M annual cost savings from consolidated infra.

## Recommendation rationale

The combination of clean financials, proven customer love, modest tech debt, in-line valuation, and complementary product positioning makes this the strongest tuck-in we've seen this year. Risk-adjusted IRR estimate is in the 28-34% range over the 4-year hold.

We recommend the executive committee approve issuing the LOI at $84M.

- **1Password pricing** was extracted from embedded JSON data in the pricing page HTML, as the page is JavaScript-rendered. The JSON contained explicit pricing details for all tiers.
- **Bitwarden pricing** was extracted from visible text in the pricing page HTML. Teams ($4/user/month) and Enterprise ($6/user/month) pricing were clearly stated.
- **LastPass Business pricing** is not publicly listed on any LastPass page. The pricing page only shows personal/family tiers. Business pricing requires contacting sales.

- **Keeper Business pricing** was found in a ZDNet comparison article ($7/user/month, minimum 5 seats). Keeper's own pricing pages did not contain explicit Business tier pricing.
- **Security incident history** for LastPass was sourced from Wikipedia, which aggregates news reports and official statements.

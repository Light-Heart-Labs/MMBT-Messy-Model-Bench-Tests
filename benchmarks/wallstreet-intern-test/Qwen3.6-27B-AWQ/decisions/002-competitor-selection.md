# ADR-002: Competitor Selection for Comparable Analysis

**Date:** 2025-06-18
**Status:** Accepted

## Context
Need to select comparable companies for relative valuation of GitLab.

## Alternatives Considered
1. **Direct competitors only:** GitHub (private, owned by MSFT), Bitbucket (Atlassian), self-hosted alternatives
2. **SaaS infrastructure peers:** Datadog, MongoDB, Confluent, Snowflake, Zscaler
3. **Broader software peers:** Atlassian, Okta, Bill.com, ServiceNow

## Decision
**Selected 8 comparable companies:** Atlassian (TEAM), Datadog (DDOG), MongoDB (MDB), Confluent (CFLT), Snowflake (SNOW), Zscaler (ZS), Okta (OKTA), Bill.com (BILL)

## Rationale
1. **SaaS business model:** All are subscription-based software companies
2. **Similar growth profiles:** Revenue growth 10-30%, comparable to GitLab's 23%
3. **Publicly traded:** All have accessible financial data
4. **Mix of profitable and growth-stage:** Provides range of multiples
5. **Excluded GitHub:** Not publicly traded (owned by Microsoft)
6. **Excluded ServiceNow:** Too large ($140B+), different market position
7. **Excluded Palo Alto Networks:** Different sector (cybersecurity vs dev tools)

## Limitations
- No perfect comparable exists for GitLab's unique position in the DevOps/developer tools market
- GitHub is the closest competitor but is not publicly traded
- Self-hosted/open-source alternatives (Gitea, Gogs) are not quantifiable

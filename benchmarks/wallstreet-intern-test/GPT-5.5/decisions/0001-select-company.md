# ADR 0001: Select YETI Holdings as Memo Company

Date: 2026-04-27

## Status

Accepted.

## Context

The requested company must be publicly traded in the United States with a market capitalization between $1B and $10B. The company also needs accessible raw source material for filings, transcripts, press releases, and model inputs.

## Alternatives Considered

- YETI Holdings: consumer-products company, current market-cap screens around $3B, clean SEC history, company-hosted transcripts found in search results.
- Upwork: marketplace model with accessible filings but more normalization required around GAAP profitability and marketplace metrics.
- Sweetgreen: potentially interesting unit-growth model but more restaurant-specific source work and less certain current market-cap fit.

## Decision

Use YETI Holdings, Inc. (`YETI`). The source pull confirmed current market capitalization near $3.0B and available SEC filings, earnings releases, and company-hosted transcript PDFs.

## Consequences

The model will emphasize revenue by product/channel/geography, gross margin, SG&A leverage, inventory and working-capital normalization, share repurchases, and DCF / trading-multiple valuation.

# ADR 001: Company Selection - DocuSign, Inc. (DOCU)

**Date**: 2024-10-01  
**Status**: Approved

## Alternatives Considered

1. **UiPath (PATH)** - $5.4B market cap, RPA software
2. **DocuSign (DOCU)** - $9.0B market cap, e-signature and agreement automation

## Decision

Selected **DocuSign (DOCU)** for the following reasons:

- **Market Leadership**: DocuSign is the clear market leader in e-signature with ~80% market share
- **Expansion into Agreement Lifecycle**: Successfully transitioning from e-signature to broader agreement automation platform
- **Enterprise Adoption**: Strong presence in Fortune 500 companies with high retention rates
- **Financial Health**: Positive cash flow, growing subscription revenue, improving margins
- **Digital Transformation Tailwinds**: Long-term secular trend toward digital workflows

## Rationale

While UiPath is an interesting AI/automation play, DocuSign offers:
- More predictable subscription revenue model
- Proven monetization of core product
- Clear path to $1B+ annual revenue with 30%+ operating margins
- Less competitive intensity than RPA space

## Exit Criteria

If DocuSign fails to show meaningful progress in expanding beyond e-signature into full agreement lifecycle management, reconsider UiPath or other candidates.

# ADR 002: Valuation Multiples Selection

**Date**: 2024-10-02  
**Status**: Approved

## Multiples Analyzed

1. **EV/Revenue**: 6.4x (EV $8.3B / Revenue $3.22B)
2. **EV/EBITDA**: 8.5x (EV $8.3B / EBITDA $975M)
3. **P/E**: 31.2x (Price $55 / EPS $1.76)
4. **FCF Yield**: 12.1% (FCF $1.06B / EV $8.3B)

## Decision

Selected **EV/Revenue multiple of 6.4x** for the following reasons:

- **Revenue growth focus**: DocuSign is transitioning from e-signature to agreement automation, making revenue growth the key metric
- **Industry comparison**: Adobe trades at 4.1x EV/Revenue, Salesforce at 3.5x, but with higher margins
- **Growth premium**: DocuSign's 8% growth justifies a premium to peers
- **FCF yield validation**: 12.1% FCF yield is attractive but volatile due to working capital swings

## Alternative Considered

**P/E multiple of 25x** (normalized earnings) would imply $44/share, but this doesn't account for growth potential in agreement automation market.

## Exit Criteria

If DocuSign fails to grow revenue to $4B+ by 2027, reconsider multiples and potentially exit position.

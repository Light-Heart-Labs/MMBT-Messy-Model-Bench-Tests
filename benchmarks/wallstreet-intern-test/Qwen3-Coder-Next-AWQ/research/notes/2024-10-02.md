## Research Questions

### Q1: What is DocuSign's revenue breakdown by product?
**Answer**: From 10-K, Subscription revenue = $3.15B (97.8% of total), Professional services = $68.9M (2.2%)

### Q2: What is DocuSign's customer retention rate?
**Answer**: From 10-K, "We have historically maintained high customer retention rates" but specific NRR not disclosed. From earnings call, "NRR remains above 120%"

### Q3: What is DocuSign's competitive advantage?
**Answer**: 
- Network effects (more users = more value)
- Brand recognition ("to sign" = "to DocuSign")
- Integration ecosystem (1000+ integrations)
- Regulatory compliance (global e-signature laws)
- AI-native IAM platform leadership

### Q4: What is the addressable market?
**Answer**: 
- E-signature market: ~$10B TAM
- Agreement automation: ~$15B TAM
- IAM market: ~$20B TAM
- DocuSign's TAM: ~$20-25B with expansion opportunity

### Q5: What are the key risks?
**Answer**:
- Dependence on e-signature market (need to assess expansion success)
- Competition from Microsoft, Adobe, HelloSign
- Customer concentration (enterprise customers)
- Working capital management (current ratio 0.73)
- Integration of acquired companies (Lexion)
